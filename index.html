Nemo

selenium-webdriver automation for node.js

Nemo provides enough abstraction to remove the pain while retaining all of the power of selenium-webdriver. Wrap Nemo with your favorite test framework and task runner, add and author plugins that complete your testing requirements.

Use generator-nemo to add Nemo to an existing app
